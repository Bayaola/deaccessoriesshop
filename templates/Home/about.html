ABOUT US

Design Genius is a Startup Based in Cameroon. We Design and Build Machines that solves Problems in Africa (Products for Africa by Africans). These machines increase productivity while reducing stress in domains such as Agriculture, Food processing and the Transformation sector using Numerical Fabrication with IoT(Internet of Things). To Prevent Companies, Businesses or individuals from spending years Developing their team for Product Design and Development of new Product or Existing Products, we solve the problem through Engineering, Product Design, Prototyping and testing, with Manufacturing and marketing support. We help you take your product to the market as quick as possible.
