Sample Projects

Automatic hand washing Machine

During the Covid-19 Virus outbreak our team (Design Genius) with the support of ENSAI (National school of Agro-Industrial Sciences)and the University of Ngaoundere Decided to stop the propagation of Covid-19 in the University of Ngaoundere by Designing and Fabricating 09 Touch Free, Fully Automated Handwashing Machines. This machine had 1000, 250 and 50 hand washing cycle Capacity , Fast and easy with a 20 Seconds washing cycle to meet the CDC (Disease Control and Prevention) requirement. This was done under a Month with maintenance support for one year.
